Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 01, 2025

1) Who we are

Controller: Individual Entrepreneur Daud Gazizullin
Email: pandora.appbox@proton.me

2) What information we process

  • Account identifiers: email address and authentication ID (UUID) when you sign in with Google or another provider.
  • App content: habits, categories, contexts, schedules, and logs you create inside the app.
  • Technical data: timestamps and minimal diagnostic logs needed for security and reliability.

3) Where data is stored

  • On your device: all habit data is stored locally in an encrypted database (Drift/SQLite).
  • Cloud backend: if you enable sync, data is transmitted securely to our self-hosted backend running on cloud infrastructure located in the EU or other regions offering comparable protection. The provider may change, but data is always processed under our control.

4) Purposes and legal bases

  • Provide, maintain, and sync the app (performance of contract).
  • Secure accounts and prevent abuse (legitimate interest).
  • Send optional notifications (consent, which you may withdraw).

5) Sharing and processors

We do not sell or rent your data. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (for example, cloud hosting, email delivery, or database management) solely to operate the service, bound by data-processing agreements. Authentication via Google uses Google LLC as an identity provider under their Privacy Policy.

6) International transfers

When data leaves Georgia or the EEA, we rely on contractual safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses. Hosting regions are selected to provide GDPR-level protection.

7) Retention

  • Local data: remains until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud data: retained while your account is active and deleted upon request or account removal.
  • Logs: kept briefly for security and troubleshooting, then deleted or anonymized.

8) Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data at any time. To exercise these rights, email pandora.appbox@proton.me. You can also contact the Personal Data Protection Service of Georgia (personaldata.ge) or, if you are an EU resident, your local supervisory authority.

9) Security

Data in transit is protected by HTTPS/TLS. Server access is restricted and monitored. We apply least-privilege principles and regular updates to reduce risk.

10) Children

The app is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

11) Changes

We may revise this policy. The effective date above shows the latest version. Material updates will be announced in-app or on our website.

12) Contact

Questions or requests: pandora.appbox@proton.me
