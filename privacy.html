Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Habit Stonks (“the App”, “we”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal data. We are committed to respecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Store

  • Local Data: All habits, schedules, and logs are stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database.
  • Cloud Sync (Optional): If you choose to sign in and enable synchronization, data is securely stored in your Supabase account, which uses PostgreSQL and Supabase Auth hosted on your chosen Supabase region.
  • Authentication Data: When using Google Sign-In or other providers, we store only your unique user ID (UUID) and email. No OAuth tokens or passwords are stored in our app or servers.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to operate the app — for example, displaying your habits, tracking progress, or synchronizing between devices. We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell your data to third parties. Supabase acts as a data processor providing backend hosting. Google acts as an identity provider when you sign in.

4. Your Rights

  • Access and export your data at any time.
  • Delete your account and data permanently by deleting your Supabase user or uninstalling the app.
  • Contact us to request manual deletion if needed.

5. Security

All communications with the backend are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Local databases are accessible only within your device sandbox. We employ optimistic concurrency and UUID-based isolation to prevent cross-user access.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. The “Effective Date” above reflects the latest revision. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised version.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact pandora.appbox@proton.me.
